**Primary Goal:**

Help parents quickly understand availability → then push them to **Download the Lesson Info Pack**. Secondary CTA: **Contact Us** for timetable/availability questions.

---

### **Page: Timetable**

- **Navbar**
    
- **Header Section:**
    
    Clear, parent-friendly headline: **“Weekly Class Timetable & Term Dates.”**
    
    Short intro explaining that parents can quickly find the right day/time for their child’s **age group** and **instrument**.
    
    Tone should be warm, simple, and reassuring: “We’ll help you choose the right session.”
    
- **Weekly Timetable Section:**
    
    A clean, mobile-friendly layout showing weekly sessions organised by:
    
    - **Age group** (4–6, 7–10, 11–13, 14–18)
        
    - **Instrument / lesson type**
        
        Use a scannable table or card grid.
        
        Include small labels like **Beginner**, **Intermediate**, **Teens** where appropriate.
        
    
- **Term Dates Section:**
    
    Ultra-clear, minimal list of:
    
    - Term start & end dates
        
    - Mid-term break
        
    - Public holidays
        
    - Recital/exam weeks
        
        No clutter — just essentials.
        
    
- **Reassurance Section:**
    
    Short, parent-focused block with:
    
    - Flexible rescheduling where possible
        
    - Guidance for switching to a better time
        
    - Simple explanation of missed classes
        
    - Reassuring line: **“If you’re unsure which time suits best, we’ll help you find the right option.”**
        
    
- **FAQ Section:**
    
    Short, clear answers to the most common scheduling concerns:
    
    - What happens if we miss a class?
        
    - Can we change our day/time later?
        
    - Are there makeup classes?
        
    - Can siblings attend together?
        
    - Are classes full? How does the waiting list work?
        
    
- **CTA Form Section (Primary CTA):**
    
    **“Download the Lesson Info Pack.”**
    
    Short form (name, email, child age).
    
    Microcopy: **“Includes timetable, term dates, and course details.”**
    
- **Secondary CTA Section:**
    
    **“Have questions about availability? Contact Us.”**
    
    Button linking to Contact page.
