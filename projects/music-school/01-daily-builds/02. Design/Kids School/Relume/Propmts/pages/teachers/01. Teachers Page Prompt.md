**Primary Goal:**

Build trust instantly through real teachers, safety standards, and qualifications — then push parents to **Download the Lesson Info Pack**. Secondary CTA: **Contact Us**.

  

**Page Requirements:**

- **Navbar**
    
- **Header Section:**
    
    Warm, reassuring headline introducing the teaching team.
    
    Short intro emphasising:
    
    - Child-friendly teaching
        
    - Safety and safeguarding
        
    - Qualifications and experience
        
    - The school’s commitment to trusted educators
        
        Include a real classroom/teacher photo.
        
    
- **Team Section:**
    
    Grid of teacher profiles featuring:
    
    - Real teacher photos
        
    - Name
        
    - Instruments taught
        
    - Short warm bio
        
    - Key trust points: years teaching children, exam prep experience, friendly & encouraging style
        
        Focus on warmth and safety.
        
    
- **Qualifications & Safety Section (Features List):**
    
    Scannable trust signals:
    
    - Music degrees + professional training
        
    - Garda vetting / background checks
        
    - Child protection training
        
    - First aid (if applicable)
        
    - Ongoing professional development
        
    - Experience with children of all ages
        
    
- **Safeguarding Commitment Section (Feature Block):**
    
    Dedicated reassuring section explaining:
    
    - School’s safeguarding standards
        
    - Safe classroom environments
        
    - Supervision rules
        
    - Child-first teaching philosophy
        
    - How parents can raise concerns
        
        Warm, parent-focused tone.
        
    
- **Testimonial Section:**
    
    Parent testimonials specifically praising teachers.
    
    MUST include:
    
    - Teacher name
        
    - Child age
        
    - A specific positive outcome (confidence, progress, enjoyment)
        
    
- **CTA Form Section (Primary CTA):**
    
    **Download the Lesson Info Pack**
    
    Fields: name, email, child age
    
    Reassurance line:
    
    “Meet the teachers and learn how we support your child’s progress.”
    
- **Secondary CTA Section:**
    
    “Prefer to talk to us? Contact Us.”
    
    Button links to Contact page.
    
- **Footer**
    

  

**Tone:**

Warm, trustworthy, parent-focused, safe, professional. No jargon.
