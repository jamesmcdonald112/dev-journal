**Primary Goal:**

Convert parents into enquiries through the **Lesson Info Pack download form** (main funnel) or the **Contact Us** page (secondary funnel).

  

**Page Requirements:**

- **Hero Section:** Warm, professional, parent-focused hero with a real photo of children in lessons. Clear value proposition (safe, fun, structured progress). Primary CTA: **Download Lesson Info Pack**.
    
- **Age Groups Overview:** Clear, scannable blocks explaining lesson options by age:
    
    Ages 4–6, 7–10, 11–13, 14–18.
    
    Short, parent-friendly descriptions for each.
    
- **Pricing Preview:** Transparent, simple pricing highlight (“From €X per term”). Link to full Pricing page. Keep the preview short and reassuring.
    
- **Key Benefits:** Parent-focused benefits: qualified teachers, child-friendly approach, small groups, safety-first environment, real progress.
    
- **Testimonials:** Real parent testimonials with child ages. Use friendly, specific quotes that mention confidence, progress, and teacher quality.
    
- **About Block:** Short introduction to the school’s mission, teaching philosophy, and credentials — warm and trustworthy. Include a real classroom/teacher photo.
    
- **Info Pack Form:** Prominent, friction-free form placed on the Home page. Fields: name, email, child age. Microcopy: “Takes 30 seconds — includes pricing, timetable, and lesson guide.”
    
- **Secondary CTA:** “Ready to enrol? Contact Us” — linking to the Contact page.
    
- **Visual Style:** Real photography only, warm colours, clean layout, mobile-first design. Keep sections short and easy to scan.
    

  

**Tone:**

Warm, professional, reassuring, parent-focused. Short, clear copy. Avoid music jargon.

  

**Do NOT include:**

Blog, news, long text blocks, or anything that distracts from conversion.
