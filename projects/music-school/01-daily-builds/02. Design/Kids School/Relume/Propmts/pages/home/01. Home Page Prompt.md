# **Home Page Prompt (High-Converting Kids Music School)**

  

**Primary goal:**

Convert parents into enquiries by pushing them to **Download the Lesson Info Pack** (main funnel) or use the **Contact Form** (for high-intent parents).

  

**Page requirements:**

- Warm, professional, reassuring hero section with a real photo of children in lessons
    
- Clear value proposition for parents (safety, progress, fun learning)
    
- Simple explanation of lesson options and age groups (ages 4–6, 7–10, 11–13, 14–18)
    
- **Pricing preview** showing transparent starting prices (“From €X per term”)
    
- Highlight key parent benefits (qualified teachers, small groups, safe environment)
    
- Real parent testimonials with child ages
    
- Short “About the School” block
    
- **Lesson Info Pack download form directly on the Home page** (name, email, child age)
    
- Secondary CTA to the Contact page
    
- Real photos of kids, teachers, and classrooms
    
- Mobile-friendly layout, short sections, clear hierarchy
    

  

**Tone:**

Warm, professional, parent-focused, reassuring. Avoid jargon.

  

**Do NOT include:**

Blog, news, or long text sections.
