**Structure:**

- **Navbar**
    
- **Header Section:**
    
    Warm, clear headline: “Simple, Transparent Pricing for Every Family.”
    
    Short intro explaining how pricing works (per term or per lesson), what’s included, and reassurance that there are no hidden fees.
    
- **Pricing Section:**
    
    Clean pricing table/cards with clear breakdowns:
    
    - Group lessons (price per term / per week)
        
    - Private lessons (price per term / per week)
        
    - Optional extras (e.g., exam prep, instrument hire — only if applicable)
        
        Highlight what’s included in each option: lesson length, materials, progress tracking, recitals, etc.
        
    
- **Benefits Section:**
    
    Parent-focused reasons the pricing offers value — qualified teachers, structured curriculum, small groups, safe environment, progress reports.
    
- **Discounts Section:**
    
    Optional small block for sibling discounts or multi-class discounts (if the school offers them).
    
- **FAQ Section:**
    
    Short, clear answers to the biggest pricing-related questions:
    
    - How payments work
        
    - Refund/cancellation policy
        
    - Missed class rules
        
    - Do we pay per term?
        
    - Is there a deposit?
        
    
- **CTA Form Section (Primary CTA):**
    
    “Download the Lesson Info Pack” — short form (name, email, child age).
    
    Reassurance microcopy: “Full pricing, timetable, and lesson details included.”
    
- **CTA Section (Secondary CTA):**
    
    “Want to ask about availability? Contact Us.” — button linking to Contact page.
    
- **Footer**
