# **Page: About — Final Prompt**

  

**Create an About page that builds trust with parents and humanises the school.**

**Tone: warm, professional, reassuring, and child-focused.**

**Keep all sections short, scannable, and benefit-driven.**

---

### **Header Section**

  

Warm headline introducing the school.

Short intro explaining the school’s purpose: helping children aged 4–18 grow in confidence, creativity, and musical skill in a safe, structured, friendly environment.

Use a real classroom or recital photo.

---

### **About Section (Story)**

  

Tell the story of the school in simple, parent-friendly language: why it was founded, what the school believes about teaching children, and its role in the local community.

Avoid jargon; focus on warmth, safety, and genuine passion for music education.

---

### **Mission & Philosophy Section**

  

Explain the school’s mission (confidence, creativity, progress, joy).

State the teaching philosophy: child-centred, encouraging, structured, and supportive.

Make it easy for parents to understand how children learn best here.

---

### **Credentials & Trust Section**

  

List recognisable trust signals:

- Teacher qualifications
    
- Garda vetting / safeguarding training
    
- Music education credentials
    
- Years in operation
    
- Local recognition or partnerships
    
    Use icons or a tidy layout for clarity.
    

---

### **Team Intro Section**

  

Short introduction to the teaching team with a group photo.

Include a CTA link to the full Teachers page.

Focus on warmth, expertise, child-friendliness, and safety.

---

### **Gallery Section (Optional)**

  

A few real photos (classrooms, lessons, recitals) to show authenticity, joy, safety, and engagement.

---

### **CTA Form Section**

  

Primary CTA: “Download the Lesson Info Pack” — include short form (name, email, child age).

Reassurance line: “Includes pricing, timetable, lesson guide, and teacher info.”

---

### **CTA Section (Secondary)**

  

“Want to speak to us directly? Contact Us.”

Link to Contact page.
