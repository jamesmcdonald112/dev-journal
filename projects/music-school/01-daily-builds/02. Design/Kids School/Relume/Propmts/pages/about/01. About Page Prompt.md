# **Page: About — Final Prompt**

  

**Create an About page that builds trust with parents and humanises the school.**

**Tone: warm, professional, reassuring, and child-focused.**

**Keep all sections short, scannable, and benefit-driven.**

---


- **Navbar**
    

---

- **Header Section:**
    
    Warm headline and brief intro:
    
    **“A Place Where Your Child’s Confidence and Creativity Grow.”**
    
    Short paragraph introducing the school’s mission to nurture children (ages 4–18) in a safe, structured, friendly music environment.
    
    Include real classroom or recital photo.
    
    Add a reassurance line: **“Beginners welcome — no experience or instrument needed.”**
    

---

- **About Section:**
    
    The school’s story: why it was founded, passion for child-centred music education, and commitment to the local community.
    
    Parent-friendly language (no jargon).
    
    Emphasise warmth, safety, and genuine care for children.
    

---

- **Mission & Philosophy Section:**
    
    Explain mission: **confidence, creativity, progress, joy**.
    
    Explain teaching philosophy: **child-centred, structured, encouraging, supportive**.
    
    Make it simple so parents understand why children thrive here.
    

---

- **Stats Section: Credentials & Trust:**
    
    Icons + short text showing:
    
- Teacher qualifications
    
- Garda vetting / safeguarding
    
- Music education credentials
    
- Years of operation
    
- Local partnerships / recognition
    

  

Clear, scannable trust signals.

---

- **Team Section:**
    
    Short intro to the teaching team with group photo.
    
    Focus: warmth, expertise, child safety.
    
    CTA link: **“Meet Our Teachers”** → Teachers page.
    

---

- **Gallery Section:**
    
    A few real photos of classrooms, lessons, and recitals to show joy, progress, safety, and engagement.
    

---

- **CTA Form Section:**
    
    Primary CTA: **“Download the Lesson Info Pack”**
    
    Short form (name, email, child age).
    
    Reassurance: **“Includes pricing, timetable, lesson guide, and teacher info.”**
    

---

- **CTA Section:**
    
    Secondary CTA: **“Want to speak to us directly? Contact Us.”**
    
    Link to Contact page.
    

---
