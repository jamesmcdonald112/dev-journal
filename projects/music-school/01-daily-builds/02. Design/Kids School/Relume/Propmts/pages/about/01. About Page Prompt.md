
**Goal:**

Humanise the school, build deep trust, demonstrate credibility, and push parents toward the **Lesson Info Pack**.

  

**Tone:**

Warm, professional, reassuring, parent-focused. No jargon. Short sections. Scannable.

---

## **Page Structure**

  

### **Navbar**

---

### **Header Section**

  

Warm headline:

  

**“A Place Where Your Child’s Confidence and Creativity Grow.”**

  

Short intro describing the school’s commitment to nurturing children (ages 4–18) in a safe, structured, welcoming music environment.

  

Include a real photo of a classroom or recital.

  

Reassurance line:

**“Beginners welcome — no experience or instrument needed.”**

---

### **About Section**

  

Tell the school’s story in a warm, parent-friendly way:

- Why the school was founded
    
- Passion for child-centred music education
    
- Commitment to safety, encouragement, and positive childhood experiences
    
- Strong connection to the local community
    

  

Avoid all technical music jargon.

---

### **Mission & Philosophy Section**

  

Short, clear explanations of:

- **Mission:** confidence, creativity, progress, joy
    
- **Teaching philosophy:** child-centred, structured, encouraging, supportive
    

  

Explain how and why children thrive here (simple, reassuring language).

---

### **Credentials & Trust Section (Stats Block)**

  

Show clear trust signals using icons + short text:

- Teacher qualifications
    
- Garda vetting / safeguarding compliance
    
- Music education credentials
    
- Years of operation
    
- Local partnerships or recognition
    

  

Make it highly scannable.

---

### **Team Section**

  

Short intro to the teaching team with a group photo.

  

Focus on:

- Warm, friendly teachers
    
- Child safety and training
    
- Expertise in working with young learners
    

  

Include CTA link:

**“Meet Our Teachers” → Teachers Page**

---

### **Gallery Section**

  

A small gallery of real photos showing:

- Lessons
    
- Recitals
    
- Kids engaged and smiling
    
- Warm, safe classroom spaces
    

  

Authenticity only — no stock images.

---

### **CTA Form Section (Primary CTA)**

  

**“Download the Lesson Info Pack”**

  

Form fields:

- Name
    
- Email
    
- Child Age
    

  

Reassurance microcopy:

**“Includes pricing, timetable, lesson guide, and teacher information.”**

---

### **CTA Section (Secondary CTA)**

  

**“Want to speak to us directly? Contact Us.”**

Links to Contact Page.

---
