
**Goal:**

Show upcoming events in a warm, parent-friendly way that builds trust and encourages engagement.

Keep everything simple, emotional, and community-focused.

Primary funnel: **Download Lesson Info Pack**.

---

### **Navbar**

---

### **Header Section**

  

Warm, inviting headline:

**“Upcoming Events, Concerts & Workshops.”**

  

Short intro explaining that events help children:

- build confidence
    
- celebrate progress
    
- feel part of a community
    

  

Reassurance:

**Beginners are always welcome — events are fun, supportive, and inclusive.**

  

Include a real event or recital photo.

---

### **Events List Section**

  

Clean, scannable cards or list showing upcoming events such as:

- Recitals
    
- Open Days / Trial Class Days
    
- Camps & Workshops (e.g., Summer Music Camp)
    
- Exam Preparation Sessions
    
- Holiday or Themed Events
    

  

Each event card must include:

- title
    
- date
    
- short description
    
- age group
    
- important parent notes (e.g., “family welcome,” “bring instrument,” “free entry,” etc.)
    

  

Cards must be easy to scan on mobile.

---

### **Gallery Section**

  

A small gallery of **real photos** from past events:

- concerts
    
- group workshops
    
- summer camps
    
- recitals
    

  

Purpose: reinforce trust, show joy, engagement, and real student progress.

---

### **Feature Section — Why Events Matter**

  

Short, emotional parent-focused benefits:

- builds confidence
    
- helps children perform in a safe environment
    
- creates friendships
    
- motivates practice at home
    
- brings families into the learning experience
    

  

Simple language, warm tone.

---

### **FAQ Section**

  

Brief, clear answers to the most common questions:

- “Can beginners join events?”
    
- “Does my child have to perform?”
    
- “Is there a fee?”
    
- “Can family members attend?”
    
- “How do we sign up for events?”
    

  

Each answer should be short and reassuring.

---

### **CTA Form Section — Primary CTA**

  

**“Download the Lesson Info Pack.”**

Short form (name, email, child age).

Reassurance line:

**“Includes pricing, timetable, lesson details, and event information.”**

### **CTA Section — Secondary CTA**

Button linking to the Contact page.
