**Primary Goal:**

Guide parents to understand age groups and lesson types, reduce uncertainty, and push them to **Download the Lesson Info Pack**. Secondary CTA: **Contact Us**.

  

**Page Requirements:**

- **Header Section:** Warm, welcoming headline:
    
    **“Find the Perfect Music Lesson for Your Child — by Age & Instrument.”**
    
    Short intro explaining that lessons are grouped by age, tailored to each stage, and that **beginners are always welcome**. Reassure parents: “If you’re unsure which lesson suits your child, we’ll guide you.”
    
- **Age Groups Section:**
    
    Four clear, scannable blocks:
    
    - Ages 4–6 → musical play, rhythm, early confidence
        
    - Ages 7–10 → foundations, fun learning, building skills
        
    - Ages 11–13 → technique, teamwork, progression
        
    - Ages 14–18 → advanced skills, exam prep, performance
        
        Parent-friendly, benefit-driven descriptions.
        
    
- **Lesson Types Section:**
    
    Piano, Guitar, Voice/Singing, Drums, Violin, Group Music, Private Tuition.
    
    For each: 1–2 parent-focused benefits (“Builds confidence”, “Great for beginners”, “Supports focus & creativity”) + who it’s suitable for.
    
- **How It Works Section:**
    
    Simple steps: weekly lessons → small groups/private → qualified teachers → supportive progress tracking → no instrument required to start.
    
- **Benefits Section:**
    
    Emotional and parent-focused benefits: confidence, creativity, focus, musicality, week-by-week progress, enjoyment.
    
- **Gallery Section:**
    
    Real photos of children learning, interacting with teachers, practising instruments — no stock imagery.
    
- **FAQ Section:**
    
    Common parent questions:
    
    - Do we need our own instrument?
        
    - Are beginners welcome?
        
    - How are groups chosen?
        
    - Can my child switch instruments?
        
    - What if we miss a class?
        
    
- **Info Pack CTA Form Section:**
    
    Primary CTA: **Download the Lesson Info Pack**.
    
    Short fields: name, email, child age.
    
    Reassurance line: “Takes 30 seconds — includes pricing, timetable, and age-group guidance.”
    
- **Secondary CTA Section:**
    
    “Still have questions? Contact Us.” Button links to Contact page.
    
- **Footer**
    

  

**Tone:**

Warm, professional, parent-focused, reassuring. Clear benefits, no jargon.
