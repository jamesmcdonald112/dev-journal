# **📌 Lessons Page Prompt (Final Version)**

  

**Page Goal:**

Help parents quickly understand lesson options by age and instrument, reassure them that beginners are welcome, and drive them to download the Lesson Info Pack.

  

**Prompt:**

“Create a parent-focused Lessons page that clearly explains the school’s lesson options for children aged 4–18. Include a warm, welcoming header: ‘Find the Perfect Music Lesson for Your Child — by Age & Instrument.’ Add a short introduction explaining that lessons are grouped by age, tailored to each stage, and that beginners are always welcome.

  

Include two clear feature sections:

1. **Age Groups:** Ages 4–6 (musical play & rhythm), Ages 7–10 (foundations & fun), Ages 11–13 (technique & teamwork), Ages 14–18 (advanced skills & exam prep).
    
2. **Lesson Types:** Piano, Guitar, Voice/Singing, Drums, Violin, Group Music, Private Tuition — each with 1–2 parent-friendly benefits and who the lesson is suitable for.
    

  

Add a ‘How It Works’ section explaining weekly lessons, small groups or private options, qualified teachers, no instrument required to start, and supportive progress tracking.

  

Include a Benefits section highlighting confidence, creativity, focus, musicality, and week-by-week progress.

  

Add a small gallery of real photos of children learning and interacting with teachers.

  

Add an FAQ covering: needing an instrument, total beginners, how groups are chosen, switching instruments, and missed classes.

  

Primary CTA: **Download the Lesson Info Pack** with a short form (name, email, child age) and a reassurance line (‘Takes 30 seconds — we’ll help you choose the right lesson.’)

  

Secondary CTA: **Still have questions? Contact Us.**

  

Keep the layout clean, warm, and mobile-friendly.”
