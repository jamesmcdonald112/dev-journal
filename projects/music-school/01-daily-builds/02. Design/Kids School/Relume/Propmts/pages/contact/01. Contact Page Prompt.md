

**Goal:**

Make it extremely easy for parents to get in touch. Reassure them, reduce friction, and provide clear next steps. Prioritise simplicity, warmth, and fast response expectations.

---

### **Page: Contact**

- **Navbar**
    
- **Header Section:**
    
    Warm headline: “We’re Here to Help — Get in Touch Anytime.”
    
    Short intro explaining that parents can contact the school with questions about lessons, availability, pricing, or next steps. Emphasise friendliness, support, and quick responses. Include a small classroom or teacher photo for warmth.
    
- **Contact Form Section:**
    
    Main contact form with fields: name, email, phone, child’s age, and message.
    
    Add reassurance microcopy: “We reply quickly and are happy to guide you through lessons and availability.”
    
    Form must be simple, mobile-friendly, and parent-focused.
    
- **Contact Section:**
    
    Clear display of phone number, email address, and school address.
    
    Include opening hours and a short reassurance: “No question is too small — we’re here to help.”
    
- **Locations Section:**
    
    Embedded map showing school location. Add info on parking, accessibility, and public transport details.
    
- **FAQ Section:**
    
    Short, parent-focused answers to reduce hesitation:
    
    - “How soon will you reply?”
        
    - “Can I visit the school?”
        
    - “Do we need an instrument first?”
        
    - “What’s the next step after contacting you?”
        
    
- **CTA Section:**
    
    Secondary CTA: “Prefer more details first? Download the Lesson Info Pack.”
    
    Button linking to the Info Pack section/form.
    
- **Footer**
