  

### **1. Enrolment Form (high intent)**

- Conditional logic (age, instrument)
    
- Minimal fields
    
- Mobile-first
    
- Reassurance text:
    
    - “We’ll match your child with the right teacher”
        
    - “Takes less than 3 minutes”
        
    

  

### **2. Info Pack Download Form (low intent, high volume)**

- Very short fields:
    
    - Name
        
    - Email
        
    - Phone
        
    - Child’s age (optional)
        
    
- Auto-sends PDF
    
- Sends lead to you
    
- Extremely high conversion rate
    

  

Parents drop off when:

- Too long
    
- Confusing
    
- No reassurance
    
- Asking unnecessary information
    

  

Add:

“After you submit, we’ll contact you with available times.”
