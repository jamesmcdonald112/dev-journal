# **Advanced Local SEO — worth it?**

  

Yes, for solicitors it matters because clients search:

- “solicitor near me”
    
- “probate solicitor [county]”
    
- “property solicitor [town]”
    

  

### **What it includes (simple):**

1. **Google Business Profile optimisation**
    
    This is the most important part. Direct ranking impact.
    
2. **Citations**
    
    Submitting the same business info (name, address, phone) to multiple directories.
    
    This builds trust signals for Google.
    
3. **Local schema markup**
    
    Tells Google the business type: "LegalService"
    
    This helps ranking and sometimes adds rich snippets.
    
4. **Local keyword optimisation**
    
    Tuning page titles and content to match service + location.
