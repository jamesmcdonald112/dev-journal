# **DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)**

  

**This Data Processing Agreement (“Agreement”)** is entered into on **[Date]**

  

Between:

  

**[Client Name]**,

(the “Controller”)

  

and

  

**[Consultant Name]**,

(the “Processor”)

  

Together referred to as the “Parties”.

---

## **1. Purpose of this Agreement**

  

This Agreement sets out the terms under which the Processor will process personal data on behalf of the Controller in accordance with:

- Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation – GDPR)
    
- Any applicable Irish data protection laws
    

  

This Agreement forms part of and is subject to the main services agreement between the Parties.

---

## **2. Roles of the Parties**

  

2.1 The Controller determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.

  

2.2 The Processor processes personal data solely on documented instructions from the Controller.

  

2.3 Nothing in this Agreement relieves the Controller of its own direct responsibilities under GDPR.

---

## **3. Scope of Processing**

  

The Processor will process personal data only to the extent necessary to provide the following services:

- Google Ads management
    
- Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads management
    
- Analytics setup and configuration (e.g., Google Analytics)
    
- Conversion tracking implementation
    
- Lead generation campaign optimisation
    

  

Processing activities may include:

- Accessing analytics dashboards
    
- Setting up tracking pixels and tags
    
- Managing advertising audiences
    
- Reviewing campaign performance data
    
- Uploading customer data to advertising platforms (if instructed by Controller)
    

---

## **4. Categories of Data Processed**

  

Depending on the services provided, the Processor may process:

- Names
    
- Email addresses
    
- Telephone numbers
    
- Form submission data
    
- Booking information
    
- Analytics identifiers (e.g., cookies, device identifiers, pseudonymous IDs)
    
- Advertising identifiers
    
- Website usage data
    

  

The Processor does not intentionally process special category data unless explicitly instructed in writing.

---

## **5. Purpose of Processing**

  

Personal data is processed solely for:

- Delivering advertising services
    
- Measuring and optimising marketing campaigns
    
- Tracking conversions and website engagement
    
- Generating business leads for the Controller
    

  

The Processor shall not use personal data for its own purposes.

---

## **6. Duration of Processing**

  

Processing will continue for the duration of the services agreement between the Parties.

  

Upon termination, personal data will be returned or deleted in accordance with Section 13 of this Agreement.

---

## **7. Processor Obligations**

  

The Processor shall:

- Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Controller.
    
- Ensure that persons authorised to process personal data are subject to confidentiality obligations.
    
- Not disclose personal data to third parties unless authorised or legally required.
    
- Maintain appropriate technical and organisational security measures.
    
- Assist the Controller in fulfilling GDPR obligations as described below.
    

---

## **8. Confidentiality**

  

The Processor shall ensure that all personnel involved in processing personal data:

- Are bound by confidentiality obligations.
    
- Have access only to the data necessary to perform their role.
    

  

Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement.

---

## **9. Security Measures**

  

The Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organisational measures including:

- Secure account access (strong passwords, 2FA where available)
    
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
    
- Restricted access to advertising and analytics accounts
    
- Secure storage of credentials
    
- Regular software updates
    

  

The Processor shall take reasonable steps to protect personal data against accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure, or access.

---

## **10. Sub-Processors**

  

The Controller authorises the Processor to use the following sub-processors as necessary:

- Google LLC (Google Ads, Google Analytics)
    
- Meta Platforms, Inc. (Meta Ads)
    
- Hosting providers (e.g., Vercel or similar)
    
- Email or CRM platforms where applicable
    

  

These providers may process data outside the European Economic Area.

  

The Processor shall ensure that sub-processors provide appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

---

## **11. International Data Transfers**

  

Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, the Processor shall ensure that:

- Transfers rely on appropriate safeguards under GDPR.
    
- Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms are in place where required.
    

---

## **12. Assistance to the Controller**

  

The Processor shall assist the Controller, where reasonably required, in:

- Responding to data subject access requests
    
- Rectification or deletion requests
    
- Data portability requests
    
- Objection to processing
    
- Conducting Data Protection Impact Assessments (if required)
    

---

## **13. Data Breach Notification**

  

In the event of a personal data breach affecting data processed under this Agreement, the Processor shall:

- Notify the Controller without undue delay after becoming aware of the breach.
    
- Provide reasonable information about the nature and impact of the breach.
    
- Cooperate in remediation efforts.
    

---

## **14. Return or Deletion of Data**

  

Upon termination of services:

- The Processor shall delete or return personal data to the Controller, unless retention is required by law.
    
- Access to advertising and analytics accounts shall be returned to the Controller.
    
- Stored credentials shall be securely deleted.
    

---

## **15. Liability**

  

Each Party remains responsible for compliance with its respective obligations under GDPR.

  

Nothing in this Agreement limits liability beyond what is permitted under applicable law.

---

## **16. Governing Law**

  

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Ireland.
